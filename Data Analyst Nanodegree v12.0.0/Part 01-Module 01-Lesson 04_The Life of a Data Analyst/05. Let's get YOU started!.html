Let's get YOU started!!

Hopefully the stories shared by these data analysts and data scientists have motivated you as you begin on your own journey into the fields of data analysis and data science. The work that data analysts do impacts humans in the real-world: across industries, sports, medicine, non-profit organizations and retail.

We hope you feel motivated and excited about what you will be learning and what you will be able to do after completing this Nanodegree program. As you embark on this journey, feel free to revisit and listen to these stories again. If these people have made it, SO CAN YOU!!
